# Active Inference — Friston

## 1. Core Idea

Active Inference models cognition as:

> minimization of variational free energy through perception and action.

---

## 2. Inference Structure

Inference is:

- probabilistic
- generative
- embodied

---

## 3. Key Shift

Unlike classical models:

- perception is not passive
- action is part of inference

---

## 4. Regime of Validity

What counts as success:

- prediction error minimization
- model-environment alignment

Active Inference defines a regime where:

- inference = control process
- epistemology = dynamics of stability
