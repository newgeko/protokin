# Inferentialism — Brandom

## 1. Core Idea

Meaning is defined by:

> inferential role in a normative space of reasons.

---

## 2. Inference Structure

Inference is:

- normative
- socially attributed
- discursive

---

## 3. Scorekeeping

Agents track:

- commitments
- entitlements
- consequences

---

## 4. Regime of Validity

A claim is valid if:

- it fits within inferential commitments
- it is justifiable in discourse

---

## 5. Protokin Reading

Inferentialism defines:

> a purely normative inference regime independent of causal explanation.

---

## 6. Tensions

- normativity vs psychology
- social attribution vs individual cognition
