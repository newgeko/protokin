# Reading Protocol

The Protokin reading protocol is a structured method for analyzing systems of inference.

---

## 1. Descriptive Level

What does the theory describe?

- cognition?
- action?
- language?
- systems?

---

## 2. Inference Structure

How does the system define inference?

- causal?
- probabilistic?
- normative?
- embodied?

---

## 3. Regime of Validity

What counts as a valid inference?

- empirical fit?
- coherence?
- success of prediction?
- social norm?

---

## 4. Coupling Structure

How are the following linked:

- perception
- action
- model
- environment

---

## 5. Failure Modes

What breaks the system?

- noise?
- contradiction?
- underdetermination?
- saturation?

---

## 6. Comparative Position

How does this system differ from:

- Bayesian models
- inferentialism
- predictive processing
